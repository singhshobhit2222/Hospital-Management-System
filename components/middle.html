WELCOME!

Ansari Health Center, Jamia Millia Islamia

Ansari Health Center is dedicated to the service of the students and staff of jamia, offer its services free to all jamians. Ansari Health Center is a Central University health center, run by the Central Government, supplementing on its own resources. The Ansari Health Center of Jamia Millia Islamia University provides various medical services to each member of jamia either the person is student, teacher or staff. Since the integrated medical and surgical facilities are provided at no cost, the services are sought by large number of the community, particularly students and staffs of the jamia every day.

On an average Around 150 patients daily visit at the OP department, per day Around 30 admissions at the IP department, per day Around 50 surgeries under various branches, per month And the number grows every day.
